Yes Chef

A simple way to save time

Yes Chef helps me find more free time

My wife and I highly value our free time. So do most other people these days. I come from a culinary background and cooking has always been important in my life.
My wife and I try and meal plan most weeks to help give us more free time.
We both have an active lifestyle that includes biking, travel, our dogs, our friends, and our own personal journeys.
Yes Chef came from the necessity of me wanting to improve the process of meal planning in our house.
My goal is to eventually have Yes Chef be a one stop solution for us.

What does Yes Chef Do?

Yes Chef allows you to make your own favorite meal database.
You can browse and sort your meals.
If you don't feel like browsing, find a recipe at Random.
Once you've found something you like. Add it to the Meal Plan.
Eventually you will be able to not only add your recipes to a grocery cart automatically, but also send those groceries to your favorite grocery store app and either pick up your list or just have it delivered.
