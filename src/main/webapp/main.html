Yes Chef

A simple way to save time

Yes Chef helps me find more free time

My wife and I highly value our free time. As do most other people I know these days.
I come from a culinary background and cooking has always been important in my life.
My wife and I try and meal plan most weeks.
This saves us tons of time for us to focus on ourselves and each other.
We both have an active lifestyle that includes biking, travel, our dogs, our friends, and our own personal journeys.
Yes Chef came from the necessity of me not wanting to go through the effort of finding meals and building shopping lists.
Mainly the shopping list part. For some reason I just really don't enjoy making the list.
This is where Yes Chef plays a role.

What does Yes Chef Do?

Yes Chef provides meals for you to browse.
If you don't feel like browsing, find a recipe at Random.
Once you've found something you like. Add it to the Meal Plan.
Once you've made a plan you can then add everything to the grocery cart.
(Eventually YC will do the shopping for you!)
